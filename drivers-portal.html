Totali Rides Bill Of Rights.

Hello Drivers,

Welcome to the team. You aren’t just a "user" of an app anymore—you are a founding partner of a London-born rideshare startup. We understand you will be running a Big Tech App or two, in the background. We also know the frustration that comes with them: the "blind" pickups, the automated bots, and the constant fear that one grumpy passenger can ruin your livelihood. With Totali Rides , the rules have changed.

We built this platform to fix the "The Big Tech Apps frustrations." Here is our Driver Bill of Rights to you:

100% Upfront Transparency:

You will always see the exact drop-off address, the total distance, your take (always totaling 70% of the Riders Fare) , and the estimated time before you hit accept. No status levels prevail, no hidden data.

The "Human-First" Rating Policy:

We do not let bots decide your fate. We believe in the power of Plurality. We will never automatically de-rate or suspend you based on a single, unsubstantiated complaint (like "rudeness" or "smell" etc). It takes multiple independent reports within a shift, or the previous or following shift, to substantiate a claim. A single only, claim remains unverified unless substantiated .

The "No-Bot" Guarantee:

If there is a substantiated dispute, you won't get a templated email. You will talk to me—a real person right here in London who understands that sometimes a "horrible driving" complaint just means you were dodging a pothole on Adelaide Street.

Unlimited Area Preferences:

Want to stay in North London/University area all day? Do it. We don't limit your filter selections to two hours. Stay where you’re comfortable, for as long as you want.

Zero-Penalty Freedom:

You are an Independent Contractor. If a ride doesn't make sense for your wallet, don’t accept it. Your acceptance rate will never be used to punish you or hide trip details, for trying to maximize your business's profitability.

As one of our Founding partners, your feedback directly shapes how we grow. We’re here to build a business that respects your time, your car, and your expertise.

Platform Psychology

The Honest 5-Star Rating™.

At Totali, we believe a rating system should measure the quality of the service, not the size of the rider’s guilt.

The "Guilt-Adjusted" Rating Problem: In traditional rideshare apps, riders are forced to face a tip prompt before they rate. Research shows that when a rider chooses not to tip—which happens over 50% of the time—it creates a negative mental state. To justify saving that money and relieve the internal "guilt," the rider’s brain looks for a reason. They tell themselves, "The ride wasn't perfect anyway," and they lower the rating to justify their decision.

"In that system, your 4-star rating isn't a reflection of your driving; it’s a psychological cover-up for an empty tip line."

The Totali Positive-State Advantage: We’ve removed the friction. When a Totali rider reaches the end of their journey, they see a fare that is exactly what they expected. They aren't being "squeezed" for more money, so they remain in a Positive State of Mind.

When that rider is asked to rate you, they aren't looking for excuses. They are simply reflecting on the great service you provided. We call this the Honest Rating System—where a 5-star rating is a genuine "Thank You" because we treat riders fairly and they treat you honestly.

Driver Support

Everything You Need to Know

Clear answers on earnings, taxes, and how we get you paid.

We use Stripe Connect for all driver payouts. Your earnings are settled weekly and deposited directly into your linked bank account. You can track your real-time balance and upcoming transfers directly through the "Earnings" tab in the Totali Driver App.

As an Independent Contractor in Ontario, the CRA requires you to collect HST on all fares. Your 70% "Gross Take" includes this tax. For example, on a $100 fare, you receive $70.00 ($61.94 for your services + $8.06 HST). Totali automatically generates tax-compliant invoices for every ride to keep your records perfect.

You maintain your standard personal auto insurance. Totali provides the primary Commercial Fleet Insurance that covers you and your passengers while you are on a "Totali Trip," ensuring you are fully compliant with Ontario transportation regulations.

Simply upload your Vulnerable Sector Screening (VSS) and a recent Driving Abstract through the app. Because we are local to London, our verification team typically approves qualified drivers within 3 to 5 business days.
